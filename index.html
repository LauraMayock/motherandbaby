They say that it takes a village to raise a child.
We aim to make it easier to find yours.

Everyone is welcome

Our group provides informal meetings in Westport, where parents and pre-school children can socialise. Carers, grandparents, guardians and childminders are also welcome. Meeting other parents in your area with children the same age as yours can help broaden you and your baby's social circle. Parents chat, share tips and give support while their children play in a safe, warm enviroment. Plenty of toys, juice, and snacks will be provided for the children and tea and coffee for the adults.
